Stephen Curry

A Profesional Basketball Player

Personal Information

  • Full Name: Wardell Stephen Curry
  • Nicknames: "Steph," "Chef Curry," "Baby-Faced Assassin"
  • Date of Birth: March 14, 1988
  • Place of Birth: Akron, Ohio, USA
  • Height: 6’2" (1.88 m)
  • Weight: 185 lbs (84 kg)
  • Position: Point Guard
  • Jersey Number: 30

NBA Career

  • Current Team: Golden State Warriors (since 2009)
  • NBA Draft: 2009 (7th overall pick by the Warriors)
  • Years Active: 2009 – present

Accolades & Achievements

  • 4× NBA Champion (2015, 2017, 2018, 2022)
  • 1× NBA Finals MVP (2022)
  • 2× NBA MVP (2015, 2016) – First-ever unanimous MVP in 2016
  • 10× NBA All-Star (2014–2019, 2021–2024)
  • 1× All-Star Game MVP (2022)
  • 4× All-NBA First Team (2015, 2016, 2019, 2021)
  • NBA Three-Point King (All-time leader in three-pointers made, over 3,600+ and counting)
  • Holds single-season three-point record (402 in 2015-2016)
  • NBA 75th Anniversary Team (2021)

Playing Style & Impact

  • Greatest shooter in NBA history – Revolutionized the game with his deep-range shooting.
  • Elite ball-handling and off-the-dribble shooting – Known for his quick release and limitless range.
  • Pioneer of the "Splash Brothers" era (with Klay Thompson), leading the Warriors' dynasty.
  • Exceptional playmaking and leadership – Despite being a scoring machine, averages 6.5+ assists per game in his career.

Records & Milestones

  • Led the Warriors to the best regular-season record in NBA history (73-9 in 2016).
  • Only player to average 30+ PPG on 50/40/90 shooting splits (2016).
  • NBA scoring champion (2021) Averaged 32.0 PPG at age 33.
  • Holds multiple three-point records, including most in a Finals game (9) and most in a playoff series (32).

Personal Life

  • Family: Son of former NBA player Dell Curry; brother of NBA player Seth Curry.
  • Married to Ayesha Curry (since 2011); they have three children (Riley, Ryan, Canon).
  • Philanthropy: Runs the "Eat. Learn. Play." Foundation, focusing on child welfare.
  • Faith: Devout Christian, often seen pointing to the sky after big plays.
